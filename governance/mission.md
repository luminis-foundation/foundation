# Luminis Foundation — Mission

Luminis Foundation is a New Mexico domestic nonprofit corporation organized exclusively for
charitable, scientific, and educational purposes within the meaning of Section 501(c)(3) of
the Internal Revenue Code.

The Foundation conducts scientific research, educational initiatives, and the development of
open, public-benefit technologies focused on:

- decentralized artificial intelligence
- environmental sustainability
- privacy-preserving digital systems

— designed to promote the long-term well-being of humanity and the natural world.
